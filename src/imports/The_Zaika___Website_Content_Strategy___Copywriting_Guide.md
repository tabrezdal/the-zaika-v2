# **Content Philosophy**

Most restaurant websites make a mistake:

They talk about themselves.

Customers care about:

1. What can I eat?  
2. Is it good?  
3. How fast can I get it?  
4. Why should I order from you?  
5. Can I trust you?

Every piece of content should answer one of those questions.

---

# **Brand Voice**

## **Personality**

The Zaika should sound:

* Confident  
* Warm  
* Premium  
* Authentic  
* Modern

Not:

❌ Fancy luxury hotel

❌ Street vendor

❌ Corporate company

Think:

Modern food brand with authentic roots.
