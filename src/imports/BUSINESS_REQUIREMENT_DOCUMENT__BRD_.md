# **1\. Executive Summary**

The Zaika currently operates as a cloud kitchen primarily acquiring customers through third-party aggregators such as Zomato and Swiggy.

While these platforms provide visibility and customer acquisition, they create several challenges:

* High commission fees  
* No ownership of customer data  
* Limited customer retention mechanisms  
* Reduced profit margins  
* Dependence on third-party algorithms

The objective of this project is to establish a direct-to-customer digital platform that enables:

* Direct ordering  
* Customer retention  
* Loyalty programs  
* Referral systems  
* Data ownership  
* Brand building

The platform should eventually become the primary ordering channel for The Zaika.
